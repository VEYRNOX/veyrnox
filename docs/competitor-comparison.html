Where Veyrnox is alone

Zero-egress decoy sessions, decoy-PIN routing, hidden/stealth wallets and panic wipe as a coherent coercion-resistance stack. Ledger & Trezor get partial credit only via BIP-39 passphrase "hidden wallets" — a side-effect, not a duress model.

Where it's competitive

Multi-chain send (10 assets, on-chain verified), pre-sign simulation, address-poison & approval controls, WalletConnect hardening, and Secure-Enclave / StrongBox key-wrapping match or beat the mainstream field.

Where it deliberately steps back

No swap, on-ramp, staking, or NFT gallery — these are attack surface / egress the threat model rejects. Not open-source-public, and not a cold-storage device of its own.

The honest gap

Only the ECC core audit (2026-06-23) is independent. The Hardware KEK, RASP native probes, and deniability stack are internal / device-verified at most — the one hard gate still open versus every audited incumbent. RASP is also a detector, not a shield — no code obfuscation / anti-tamper today; see the full RASP vs Promon SHIELD gap analysis & roadmap.
